What the system supports

The project covers both passenger booking and bus operator management flows.

1

Passenger search

Passengers can search by source, destination, date, operating day, AC type, seating type, and safety preference.

2

Bus registration

Conductors can list buses with route details, stops, timings, total seats, and weekly or date-based schedules.

3

Booking workflow

The app creates bookings, calculates available seats, simulates payment, and stores booking history for users.

For passengers

Find buses quickly, review route timings, choose seat count, and confirm your booking after login.

For conductors

Create a conductor profile, register buses, and view schedules from a dedicated conductor dashboard.
